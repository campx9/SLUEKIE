OUR STORY

Redefining Rest

Founded in 2019, Sluekie was born from a simple yet powerful belief: that everyone deserves to experience truly transformative sleep. In a world that never stops moving, we recognized the urgent need to create a sanctuary for rest—a place where quality, science, and luxury converge to deliver unparalleled sleep experiences.

Our journey began with a critical question posed by our founding team of sleep researchers and textile innovators: What if bedding could do more than just cover you? What if it could actually enhance your sleep quality, accelerate physical recovery, regulate temperature throughout the night, and help you wake feeling genuinely refreshed and energized?

That question led us down a rigorous path of research and development, spanning over three years and countless prototypes. We forged partnerships with leading sleep scientists at renowned institutions, collaborated with master textile engineers across three continents, and consulted with wellness experts to understand the holistic nature of rest.
